About Us

Breezy Air is a new generation HVAC installation & commissioning company founded by an educated and energetic youth with more than 10+ years of experience in all aspects of the HVAC industry. We are located in the heart of NCR and are dedicated to performing at the highest esteem.

We focus on making air conditioning and ventilation systems accessible to everyone, whether in the residential, commercial, or industrial sector. Our engineering team takes pride in designing the highest quality of products that exemplify extraordinary performance. We drive innovation through strong research fundamentals and ensure that our products adhere to rigorous standards of safety and quality.

Starting in early 2018, our founder recognized the growing need for improved air conditioning & Ventilation quality in India and began investing in the research and development of Air- conditioning & Ventilation solutions with multi-brand equipments, VRF, VRV & Non- VRV. After numerous cycles of product development, our founders were able to conceive a product line specifically designed for the Indian environmental conditions in collaboration with international, core-technology providers in the HVAC industry. Thus we came in existence in the heart of the Delhi NCR with the moto : 'Committed to Excellence, Dedicated to Performance'.

Mission

Breezy Air is dedicated to providing innovative and sustainable HVAC solutions that improve the indoor air quality and comfort of our clients' buildings. We are committed to using the latest technologies and practices to deliver energy-efficient and cost-effective solutions that meet the unique needs of our clients.

Vision

Breezy Air envisions a future where every building has access to clean, comfortable, and sustainable air conditioning and ventilation. We believe that everyone deserves to breathe clean air, and we are committed to making that vision a reality.
