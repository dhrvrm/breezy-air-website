Our Approach

1

Drawing/ layout

The client shares drawings and layouts of the project. Our team interprets and analyzes them to gain a complete understanding of the requirements.

2

Design and planning

Once we have a good understanding of the project, we begin designing and planning the fittings. We finalize the drawings and share them with the client for approval.

3

Procurement

Once the drawings are approved, we procure the necessary materials.

4

Execution

Our team of engineers executes the fittings according to the approved drawings.
